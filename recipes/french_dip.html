French Dip

Mmmm... Mnnnn... Oui Oui. Foux du fafa.

You won't find this at your local boulangerie.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 kg of French Beef
  • 2.2 oz of Mimolette
  • 0.3333 oz of Herbes de Provence
  • 0.5 g of Sel de Provence
  • 1/4 kilometer of Fresh Baguette
  • 1 packet of Winco© Au Jus Powder
  • 1 carton of Gauloises (or Gitanes)

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix Herbes et Sel de Provence.
  2. Start to prepare Au Jus by following directions on Winco© Packet.
  3. Unwrap French Beef and place on nearest stone countertop and cut it into thin slices. Spread out slices on countertop for the next step.
  4. Begin the French Smoking Process:
    1. Take a cigarette from the carton of Gauloises and light it
    2. Inhale deeply and exhale on beef.
    3. Repeat until beef is cooked through
  5. Apply slight pressure to the baguette to make sure that you hear a crackle. This means its fresh.
  6. Let the ingredients rest as they are and watch Ratatouille.
  7. Cut off a small piece of your baguette (no more than 1/8 km) and cut through the middle lengthwise. Put in french-smoked beef and slices of mimolette to your taste.
  8. Ladel out Au Jus with hands.
  9. Bon Appétit!
